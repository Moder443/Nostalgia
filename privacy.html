Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Nostalgia is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and never sell your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication
  • Profile Data: Birth year, country, and gender (used to personalize nostalgic content)
  • Usage Data: Generated memories and chat conversations

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Generate personalized nostalgic content based on your era and location
  • Provide AI-powered chat conversations about memories
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates about the app (optional)

3. AI-Generated Content

All memories and chat responses in Nostalgia are generated by artificial intelligence. The content is created based on general cultural memories of specific eras and may not reflect your actual personal experiences.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information both in transit and at rest.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Clear your chat history at any time
  • Export your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, and we will remove all your personal data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Nostalgia is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
